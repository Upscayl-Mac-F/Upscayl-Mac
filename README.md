## 🖌 Overview  

Upscayl Professional Suite is an AI-powered image enhancement platform that brings neural network upscaling to a simple, accessible interface, making it practical to improve the resolution and quality of any image regardless of its original source or size.

The upscaling engine leverages GPU acceleration to process images at high speed, completing enhancement of multi-megapixel images in seconds rather than minutes on compatible hardware.

Custom model support lets advanced users import and apply specialized neural network models trained for specific image types or artistic styles, extending the app's capability beyond its built-in selection.

Side-by-side comparison view shows the original and upscaled versions simultaneously, letting you evaluate the enhancement quality before committing to saving the result.

Upscayl Professional Suite is the AI enhancement tool for users who want to recover, restore, and enlarge images with results that reflect genuine intelligence rather than simple interpolation.
